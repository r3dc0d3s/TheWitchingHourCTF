**Scenario: The Twisted Oracle**

An advanced AI, powered by a sophisticated pseudorandom number generator, claims to predict the future. It offers a glimpse into its internal workings, providing a long sequence of its outputs. However, if you can truly understand its "mind," you should be able to predict its very next thought.

**Objective:**
Connect to the oracle, observe its outputs, and predict the next value to gain access to its secrets.
